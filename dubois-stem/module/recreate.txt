Re-create the visual using pens, paper and other analog materials like markers, colored pencils, rulers, and protractors.
\n
Strive for rapid iteration, and do not focus on precision, but rather exploring the various components
of the chart, and how they are conveying the message and information. Students may work 
together or individually.
\n
At the end of the session, the re-creations may be shared and critiqued as a group.
